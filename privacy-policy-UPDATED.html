Ultimate Sports AI ("we," "us," "our") operates a sports analytics education platform. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, protect, and share your personal information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: When you sign up via Google or Apple OAuth, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from your authentication provider
  • Profile Data: Username, avatar selection, bio, preferences
  • Analytics & Picks: Your prediction history, performance statistics, coin balances (virtual currency only)
  • Payment Information: Subscription tier, payment method (processed securely by PayPal - we never store credit card numbers)
  • Communications: Messages you send through in-app chat, support tickets, feedback

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, browser type, IP address, mobile device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, features used, time spent, navigation patterns, clicks, interactions
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address (city/state level only)
  • Cookies & Tracking: Session cookies, authentication tokens, preference settings

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • OAuth Providers: Google and Apple provide basic profile information when you sign in
  • Sports Data APIs: We integrate ESPN and The Odds API for live scores and odds
  • Payment Processor: PayPal provides transaction confirmation data

  • Analyze user behavior to improve features and user experience

  • Conduct A/B testing for new features

2.3 Communications

  • Send service-related notifications (picks settled, achievements unlocked)
  • Subscription billing reminders and payment confirmations
  • Platform updates and new feature announcements

  • Send promotional emails (you can opt out anytime)

2.4 Security & Legal Compliance

  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Enforce our Terms of Service

  • Protect user safety and platform integrity

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Database Security: PostgreSQL with encrypted backups on Railway
  • Authentication: OAuth 2.0 with PKCE flow, JWT tokens with expiration
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access, role-based permissions
  • Monitoring: Real-time security alerts, rate limiting, DDoS protection
  • Payment Security: PCI-compliant payment processing via PayPal

Security Notice: No system is 100% secure. While we use best practices, we cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmission over the internet.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information as follows:

  • Active Accounts: Retained while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated, anonymized data retained up to 2 years
  • Legal/Financial Records: Subscription/payment records retained 7 years per IRS requirements
  • Backups: Deleted data may persist in backups for up to 90 days
